Who we are

Established in 1997, Alpha is a leading provider of IT solutions & consulting services to diverse global clientele. Specializing in Application Support, Software Development, Talent Acquitation, Data Analytics, Robitics Process Automation, System Integration, Infrastructure Maintenance, Cloud Migration, Compliance & Risk Analysis. Our knowledgeable staff has impressive credentials & experience in delivering superior turnkey technology solutions.

Alpha Technologies works in partnership with our clients during every phase of planning & design. Allowing them to be kept fully informed & involved during the process. Our goal is to not impose a particular solution or model, but to tailor our services to the unique needs of the client while keeping within the established budget & timeframe
